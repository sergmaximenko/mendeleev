MENDELEEV

SPEAK EASY BAR

Now table reservation + face control

The bar is a cocktail bar, so a very first thing to do, after passing strict face control, is to try and assess the fascinating work of the local mixologists, who are most of the specialist in absinte and cocktails based on absinthe.

Excellent bar menu is accompanied by light, tasty and quick to prepare Pan-Asian cuisine
from Mom — Chef next door restaurant “Roni”.

Weekdays — live piano evenings and live jazz concerts.
Weekends — cool deep techno and deep house djs

Mendeleev Bar is speak easy type of bar with the author’s cocktail menu from the famous russian mixologist Roman Milosteviy. Secret entrance is located in a tiny noodle shop “Lucky Noodles”.
